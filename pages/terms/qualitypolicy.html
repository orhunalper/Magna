Quality Policy

Magna is a company whose mission is to enhance industrial efficiency through advanced hydraulic systems and innovative engineering solutions. We specialize in delivering high-quality design services, including the design of hydraulic power units, optomechanical structures, and precision components, tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our priority is to provide solutions that support sustainable development and contribute to the success of our partners.

The path to achieving our quality objectives is based on the continuous improvement of the Quality Management System, guided by the following principles:

  • Effectiveness and innovation: We develop and implement innovative design solutions in the fields of hydraulics and engineering, adapting to evolving market demands. We continuously improve our design approach to maintain a leading position in the industry.
  • Process optimization: We focus on the design of hydraulic power units and mechanical components with an emphasis on precision and efficiency. We monitor key performance indicators and apply industry best practices to continuously enhance our design processes.
  • Training and development: We provide comprehensive training for our employees, enhancing both technical and soft skills required to deliver top-quality services. By investing in the continuous professional growth of our team, we ensure access to the latest tools and knowledge in design.
  • Customer satisfaction: Our commitment to quality begins with understanding the individual needs of our clients. Through attention to precision and detail in our designs, we deliver solutions that meet and exceed expectations, making customer satisfaction the key measure of our success.
  • Continuous improvement: We are committed to the ongoing improvement of our design and consulting processes. Through regular reviews and analysis, we aim to continually enhance the quality and efficiency of our operations.
  • Regulatory compliance: We strictly adhere to all applicable legal requirements and industry standards, ensuring that our operations are conducted ethically, transparently, and responsibly. Compliance is a foundation of our commitment to quality and reliability.

We believe that quality is embedded in every aspect of our operations, and every employee at Magna is responsible for meeting and exceeding customer expectations.
