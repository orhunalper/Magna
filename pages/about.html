    About Us

    Powerful Engineering. Tailored Solutions.

    Our aim at MAGNA is to give positive impact on worldwide as a leader of innovative hydraulic fluid power solutions for people, environment, industry and infrastructure.

    SCROLL DOWN

    WHAT WE DO

    The world is running on vital infrastructure and technology. such as aircraft, ground vehicles, underground vehicles, buildings, etc. These are crucial for day-to-day life. And the companies behind them look to us to support them in solving some of the toughest hydraulic power management challenges. Magna offers whole hydraulic and automation system solutions for fluid power management. Magna is realizing hydraulic ideas from scratch with an innovative perspective. We are aiming to produce durable, efficient, and effective products with a full focus on people's needs. We are taking care of all steps of the whole project, from beginning to end.   Because we are well aware that hydraulic power necessitates the masterful design and execution of all parts and steps.

    HOW WE WORK

    As Magna's point of view is based on the fact that every decision we make has an impact on people, the environment, and ourselves. We progressively work on finding solutions that allow us to maximize the quality and durability of our products while minimizing their negative impact on the environment and the customer. This means that we are only satisfied when we take all the steps to assure the highest quality at the lowest environmental and human cost possible. Our mission is to maintain a culture based on ethical quality in our business environment. We chose to be in partnership with our customer reach as much as possible, exactly as the final customer requirements. Only in this manner can we optimize the usage of resources and the budget.
